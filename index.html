GW CS Senior Design 2016

Christoph von Braun: DRATS

DRATS, or the Drone Recognition and Tracking Suite, is a system designed to detect errors in an autonomous drone control program. It identifies a drone using two webcams and color detection, and runs that data through an algorithm that generates an x-y-z coordinate for the drone in meters. That coordinate can then be compared to what the drone's expected location is, and be sent to a different system that can revert any detected discrepencies.

Bio: Christoph von Braun is a senior at the George Washington University with a major in Computer Science and a minor in Economics. He will be working for the government after graduation, and will remain local to the DMV area.
